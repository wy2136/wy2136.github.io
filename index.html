About

I am currently a postdoc in Prof. Gudrun Magnusdottir's group in the department of Earth System Science at UC Irvine, working on the project of impact of extreme moisture transport on Arctic sea ice.

I received my B.S and M.S. from Peking University in China, supervised by Prof. Benkui Tan; and Ph.D. in atmospheric sciences from Columbia University, with the dissertation titled The Hydroclimate of East Africa: Seasonal cycle, Decadal Variability, and Human induced Climate Change, supervised by Prof. Richard Seager and Prof. Mark A. Cane. My Ph.D. work also involved a lot of collaboration with Dr. Bradfield Lyon at IRI of Columbia University.

Research Interests

  • Moisture Transport and Arctic Climate
  • East African Hydroclimate and Drought
  • ITCZ, Global Monsoons, Storm Tracks
  • Climate Variability, Extreme Weather and Climate
